D-COLOUR FLAWLESS NATURAL DIAMONDS
The Pinnacle of Purity & Rarity
D-Color natural diamonds represent the highest grade on the IGI and GIA color scale, signifying a completely colorless appearance. These diamonds are exceptionally rare and account for less than 1% of all diamonds mined worldwide.

Composed of nearly pure carbon, these diamonds are primarily Type IIa diamonds with virtually no nitrogen impurities. Their extraordinary purity is formed over billions of years under extreme heat and pressure beneath the earth’s surface.

Due to their rarity, brilliance, and exceptional purity, D-Color Flawless diamonds are highly valued and reserved for the world’s most prestigious fine jewellery collections.
